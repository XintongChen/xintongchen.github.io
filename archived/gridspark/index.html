gridspark 360

Gridspark 360 (G360) is powerful analytics engine that sits behind gridspark's custom search toolbars. It provides toolbar owners the tools to build rich models of their users interests in order to build more relevant and engaging experiences.

The G360 platform works by anonymously collecting and analyzing toolbar user behavior data across internet properties and provides comprehensive meta-data about interests, habits and trends. These data points are combined in an easily digestable, user-friendly dashboard to provide "Suggested Content" recommendations, customer segmentation reports and recommended Google AdWords campaign optimizations. Insights captured by the G360 platform can also be directly integrated with various CMS and DSP platforms.
