Story x Museum

A mobile application that enhances the art museum experience through the telling and sharing of stories.

My Contributions

Cooperative project: Literature Review, UI/UX Design

Issues Found

Visitors to art museums are usually not satisfied with their learning experience because:

(1) the artwork tends to lack categorization and supporting information.

(2) in addition, there is low engagement with the artwork.

(3) and there is lack of interaction among visitors.

Solution

We designed a mobile application that enhances the art museum experience through the telling and sharing of stories.

(1) We categorized the artwork by themes and allowed visitors to be able to click on the artwork itself to view supporting information.

(2) Increased engagement by asking users questions about the artwork and allowing visitors to leave their responses to those questions and listen to the responses of other users.

(3) Increased social interaction amongst groups of visitors when they use the app in the museum. In addition, increased online interaction amongst visitors when they share stories and comments through the app.
