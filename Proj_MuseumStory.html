Story x Museum

A mobile App to enhance learning experience in art museums by story-telling and story-sharing.

My Contributions

Cooperative porject: Literature Review, UI/UX Design

Problem

Art museum visitors are not satisfied with their learning experience in the museum, because of (1) lack of guidance on how to appreciate art works, (2) low engagement with art work and (3) lack of social interaction among visitors.

Solution

A mobile App to enhance learning experience in art museum by story-telling and story-sharing. Visitors could see the art work, and then listen to others’ stories or tell their own stories about the art work wherever or whenever in the museum.

(1) Lack of guidance: Categorize art works by themes Open-ended scaffolding questions

(2) Low Engagement with art works: Audio recording by visitors Questions prompting users to respond

(3) Lack of Social Interaction in Museum: Face-to-face discussion with partners Learning from others through story libraries (via comments, like, listening, etc.) online
