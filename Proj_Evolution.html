Evolution!

An instructional design to teach key principles of Evolution within a simulation game.

My Contributions

Cooperative project:   Literature Review   ·   Instructional Design

Target Audiance & Learning Context

This simulation game is mainly for high school students to assist their formal learning in the biology class about the topic of Evolution.

Learning Problem

Students have intuitions that changes of traits happen naturally over time, and the evironment influences the changes of traits. But students haven't construted why these changes happen, what is the role of the environemnt, and do this changes happen in individual level or group level.

Solution

We notice that this is a complex problem. Therefore, we use Agent-Based Modeling to simulate this comples system, to help students observe the complex phenomenon. In the Agent-Based Modeling simulation, students could the change of individual and its influence on the whole system. At the same time, students could also learn by exploring, because they can manipulate single or multiple factors to explore the results.
