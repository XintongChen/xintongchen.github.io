TC Websites Redesign & Development

Technologies

Description

I work in a group of 4 to redesign and develop Teachers College websites, following the popular web design trends, e.g. cards layout, hero image, flat design, responsive design, mobile-first and so on. We develop sites within T4 Content Management System. We take advantages of T4, as well as get out of the restrictions of T4. When redesigning these websites, we satisfy the personalized requirements of different departments and also keep them consistent with the TC style.

Design Highlights

Site Name: Office of International Affairs

Requirements:

  1. Change the TC blue to a warmer theme color.
  2. Use flip card to display alumni information.
  3. Optimize card layout.

Solution:

  1. Dark red and blak/grey were chosen as theme color.
  2. New content layout (flip-card) was created in T4 system to present alumni information.
  3. 2-cards layout was used instead of 3-cards layout, to make the page looks balanced.

Site Name: Center for Technology & School Change

Requirements:

  1. Add background videos in primary carousel.
  2. A different layout and color palette from the traditional TC websites.

Solution:
