  Problem

Calligraphy is a significant part of ancient Chinese culture. The calligraphic tradition remains alive today and intrigues people from different regions. However, people sometimes do not understand the different historical backgrounds and artistic characteristics of the five basic script styles.

  Solution

This website illustrates different historical backgrounds and artistic characteristics of the five basic script styles in Chinese Calligraphy. The comparison of five script styles and the interactive question in this webpage will help people to people to remember information or rules about these script styles, as well as, make the learning process funner and more interesting.

This is a one-page project. The first part is the illustration section, and then the question section. Users could go back and forth between these two sections.
The illustration section includes: (1) what are the different artistic characteristics among five basic script styles; (2) why there are differences (historical factors that lead to these differences); (3) how these differences influence beginners’ calligraphy learning (learning tips).
In the illustration section, collapsed panel groups are used, which help users to focus on the paragraph they are reading.

The practice section includes an interactive question: matching pieces of calligraphy to five different scenarios. Learners will drag each of the five pieces of calligraphy to match five different scenarios, and website will give feedback to learners based on their answers.
