  Problem

Calligraphy is a significant part of ancient Chinese culture. The calligraphic tradition remains alive today and intrigues people from different regions. However, people sometimes do not understand the different historical backgrounds and artistic characteristics of the five basic script styles.

  Solution

I designed a webpage to teach different historical backgrounds and artistic characteristics of the five basic script styles in Chinese Calligraphy.

(1) The illustration section: I used images and text to compare different script styles and let the learners see the differences among them clearly.

(2) The game section: I designed a drag and drop game, where learners will match five pieces of calligraphy with five different characters based on the knowledge they have learned in the first section. This allows the learners to apply what they have learnt to solve problems, to strengthen their understanding of the new knowledge.
