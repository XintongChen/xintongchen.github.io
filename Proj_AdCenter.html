Ad Center

The tool for real estate agents to buy ads and manage budgets.

  Background

Corcoran's Ad Center for agents is about 20 years old and needs to be redesigned.

  Goal

Build a new Ad Center, within 2 sprints, that allows agents to intuitively and efficiently purchase advertisements and manage budgets.

  Strategy

Identify all the problems, define feature requirements and prioritize all the features for the new Ad Center.

Release the first version in the first 2 sprints, which provides agents the basic ad ordering needs.

Add new features in the following sprints to meet agents' full ad ordering, photo ordering and ad management requirements.
