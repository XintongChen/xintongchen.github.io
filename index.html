MORE ABOUT ME

I was once a lost and frustrated college student who had fears of not being good enough until I heard this:


We give up not because we don’t have the ability to reach our goals but because we cannot see the path to our destination.
- Professor Zhao

This profound quote helped me overcome my fears and truly inspired me to pursue Instructional Design, because I wanted to help students avoid the fears that I went through. Over time, I came to the realization that Instructional Design is only a subset of UX Design and that I can make a greater impact to people beyond just students.


I also love crafting CSS animations, rock climbing, painting and drawing, and photography.
